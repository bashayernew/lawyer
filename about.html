Lawyer Abdulrahman Al-Rahman

Abdulrahman Al-Rahman is the founder of Rekaz Legal Group, providing strategic legal counsel with a commitment to integrity and client success. He has represented clients across criminal, civil, and corporate matters in Kuwait.
